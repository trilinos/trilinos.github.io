## Join the TUG Meeting at HPSFcon 2026!

We will be having the Trilinos User-Developer Group (TUG) Meeting as part of the 2nd annual High Performance Software Foundation Conference (HPSFcon) in Chicago, IL on March 18-19, 2026.

We will be having
- **Introductory Talks**: presentations for new user and developers to help begin efforts using Trilnos.
- **Update Talks**: Trilinos developers covering the latest developments and features in Trilinos.
- **User Presentations**: Trilinos Users and applications sharing their recent experiences and research.

## Benefits of Attending

- Learn more about Trilinos and its capabilities.
- Network with other Trilinos Users and Developers.
- Join the HPSF community and learn about the latest trends and technologies in HPC and connect with leaders in the field.

## Call for Submissions

We are looking for presentations to the TUG Meeting, covering a wide range of topics related to Trilinos uses, development, user experiences, performance, etc. 

Submission Deadline: **January 11, 2026 at 11:59 PM CST**

Please submit your abstracts through the [HPSFcon submission system](https://events.linuxfoundation.org/hpsf-conference/program/cfp/).

⚠️  Important Note on Selecting Your Format: The conference system uses standard time blocks that include buffer time. Please select the option that best matches your intended talk length:

  * For a 10-minute Lightning Talk: Select “Session Presentation - 15 minutes”
  * For a 20-minute Full Presentation: Select “Session Presentation - 25 minutes”
  * Track: Ensure you select “Project Meeting: Trilinos” as the Topic/Track.

## HPSF Registration

To attend the TUG Meeting, [registration for HPSFcon](https://events.linuxfoundation.org/hpsf-conference/) is required.

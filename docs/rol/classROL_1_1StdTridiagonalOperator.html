Detailed Description

template<class Real>
class ROL::StdTridiagonalOperator< Real >

Provides the std::vector implementation to apply a linear operator, which encapsulates a tridiagonal matrix.

\[ T = \begin{pmatrix} a_0 & b_0 \\ c_0 & a_1 & b_1 \\ & c_1 & \ddots & \ddots \\ & & \ddots & a_{n-2} & b_{n-2} \\ & & & c_{n-2} & a_{n-1} \end{pmatrix} \]
